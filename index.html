Projects

PackBot
As a streetwear enthusiast, I have always wanted to purchase clothing from popular brands such as Supreme, but was never willing to pay the absurdly marked up prices for a resold product. That's why I created PackBot, a mobile app that utilizes web automation to purchase Supreme clothing automatically on the day of a new release. This product was designed in Swift and utilized a JavaScript backend to purchase the products, all while implementing Google Firebase to store the user's checkout information.
